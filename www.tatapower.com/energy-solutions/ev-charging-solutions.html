A

Duration of charging session depends upon multiple factors -

1. Type of charger (AC or DC): DC chargers are typically fast chargers
2. Charger rated capacity: Maximum output of charger
3. Car battery charging capacity: Maximum power at with a battery can be charged.
4. State of Charge (SOC): Existing level of charge
5. Other Parameters: Battery temperature, charger temperature etc.

Depending upon available time, you can do partial top ups (like 30 min.) or full charge (typically 1 -2 hours)
