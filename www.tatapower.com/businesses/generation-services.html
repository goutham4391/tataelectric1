Businesses - Services Business

GENERATION SERVICE

HomeBusinessesServices Business Generation Service

Tata Power Services Business provides one stop solution for all services requirements of an independent power producer (IPP), a captive power producer (CPP) or any industry requiring similar services.

Tata Power with its presence in entire value chain of power sector, has gained substantial experience and developed various processes for optimum solutions to various O&M challenges. Over the period Tata Power has adopted best in class Quality Management Systems as well as state-of-the-art technologies and digital solutions.

Our Value Proposition

  • Digitalized Product Portfolio

    Enables structured data visualization and analytics Leverages data in driving faster decision making Enables reduction in supervision & related asset management costs

  • Core Competency

    Owner's perspective in devising solutions Diversity in fleet of assets. e.g.- fuel, machines and technology used Experience - 100+ years Lead Technology Adopter

  • Focus On Customer

    Focus on Cost, time and quality Rich experience in O&M of own as well as third party generation assets Quality Systems & Processes to achieve highest quality of services to ensure long term business sustainability
